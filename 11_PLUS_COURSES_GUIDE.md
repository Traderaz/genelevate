## Exam Boards Covered

### GL Assessment
- Most common 11+ exam provider
- Used by grammar schools in Kent, Buckinghamshire, Lincolnshire, etc.
- Multiple choice format
- Covers: English, Maths, Verbal Reasoning, Non-Verbal Reasoning

### CEM (Durham University)
- Used in several counties including Yorkshire, Durham, Warwickshire
- Different question styles from GL
- Emphasis on speed
- Less predictable question formats

### ISEB (Independent Schools)
- Common Entrance Pre-Tests
- Used by many independent schools
- Similar content but different format

## Key Success Factors

### 1. Early Start
- Ideally begin preparation 12-18 months before exam
- Minimum 6 months for effective preparation
- Year 5 start recommended

### 2. Consistent Practice
- Regular short sessions better than occasional long ones
- Daily vocabulary practice
- Weekly timed practice papers

### 3. Balanced Approach
- Equal focus on all four subject areas
- Don't neglect non-verbal reasoning
- Build confidence alongside skills

### 4. Mock Exams
- Essential for time management
- Identify weak areas early
- Build exam stamina and confidence

### 5. Wellbeing
- Manage stress and pressure
- Maintain balanced lifestyle
- Ensure adequate sleep and nutrition
