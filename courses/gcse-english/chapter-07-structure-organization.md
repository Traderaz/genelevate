# Chapter 7: Structure and Organization

## Text Structure
**Beginning:** Hook reader, establish context
**Middle:** Develop ideas, build detail
**End:** Resolve, conclude, reflect

## Paragraph Structure
**Topic sentence:** Main point
**Evidence:** Quote or example
**Explanation:** Analyze/explain effect
**Link:** Connect to next paragraph

## Structural Devices
- **Flashback:** Return to earlier time
- **Foreshadowing:** Hint at future events
- **Dialogue:** Character speech
- **Shift in focus:** Zoom in/out, change perspective
- **Circular structure:** End links to beginning
- **Cliffhanger:** Unresolved ending

## Discourse Markers
- **Addition:** furthermore, moreover, additionally
- **Contrast:** however, nevertheless, whereas
- **Time:** initially, subsequently, finally
- **Example:** for instance, such as
- **Emphasis:** indeed, certainly, undoubtedly
