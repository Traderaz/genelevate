# Chapter 9: Spoken Language

## Spoken Language Endorsement
Non-examined assessment: Pass/Merit/Distinction

## Presentation (5-10 minutes)
**Choose topic:** Something you're passionate about
**Structure:**
1. Introduction (hook audience)
2. Main points (2-3 key ideas)
3. Conclusion (memorable ending)

## Techniques
- **Eye contact:** Engage audience
- **Tone/pace:** Vary for emphasis
- **Gestures:** Natural, purposeful
- **Rhetorical devices:** Questions, repetition
- **Anecdotes:** Personal stories
- **Statistics/facts:** Support points
- **Pauses:** Create impact

## Speech Writing
- Strong opening
- Clear structure
- Persuasive language
- Direct address ("You...")
- Call to action

## Responding to Questions
- Listen carefully
- Take a moment to think
- Answer clearly
- Refer back to presentation
