Master reading comprehension, creative writing, non-fiction analysis, and persuasive writing with this complete GCSE English Language revision guide aligned with AQA, Edexcel, and OCR specifications.

## Assessment Overview

**Paper 1: Explorations in Creative Reading and Writing**
- Section A: Reading (fiction extract)
- Section B: Writing (descriptive or narrative)

**Paper 2: Writers' Viewpoints and Perspectives**
- Section A: Reading (non-fiction extracts)
- Section B: Writing (viewpoint/argument)

**Spoken Language Endorsement**
- Presentation and response to questions

## Key Skills

- **AO1:** Identify and interpret information
- **AO2:** Analyze language effects
- **AO3:** Compare texts
- **AO4:** Evaluate critically
- **AO5:** Communicate clearly and creatively
- **AO6:** Use vocabulary and sentence structures accurately
