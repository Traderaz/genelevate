# Chapter 10: Exam Technique

## Paper 1: Fiction Reading & Creative Writing
**Time:** 1 hour 45 minutes
- Section A (Reading): 1 hour
- Section B (Writing): 45 minutes

**Questions:**
- Q1: List (4 marks) - 5 min
- Q2: Language (8 marks) - 12 min
- Q3: Structure (8 marks) - 12 min
- Q4: Evaluation (20 marks) - 25 min
- Q5: Creative writing (40 marks) - 45 min (5 min plan)

## Paper 2: Non-Fiction & Viewpoint Writing
**Time:** 1 hour 45 minutes
- Section A (Reading): 1 hour
- Section B (Writing): 45 minutes

**Questions:**
- Q1: Select true statements (4 marks) - 5 min
- Q2: Summary (8 marks) - 12 min
- Q3: Language analysis (12 marks) - 15 min
- Q4: Compare views (16 marks) - 20 min
- Q5: Viewpoint writing (40 marks) - 45 min (5 min plan)

## Mark Schemes
**Reading:** Levels based on analysis depth
**Writing:** 
- Content (24 marks)
- Technical accuracy (16 marks)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Not reading questions carefully
- No planning
- Copying out chunks of text
- Listing devices without explaining effects
- Poor time management
- Not checking work

## Revision Strategy
1. Practice past papers
2. Learn terminology
3. Read model answers
4. Time yourself
5. Get feedback
