# GCSE Mathematics - Complete Revision Guide

A comprehensive revision guide covering all topics in GCSE Mathematics (Foundation and Higher Tier).

## Course Overview

This course covers all major topics in GCSE Mathematics, including:
- Number operations and algebra
- Equations and inequalities
- Graphs and transformations
- Geometry and measures
- Ratio, proportion and rates of change
- Probability and statistics
- Sequences and patterns
- Trigonometry and Pythagoras
- Vectors and proof
